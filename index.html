Integrity

What are you without it? I will never trust any of the players banned for match fixing again. I don't care how young, or immature you were or still are. The fact is these players knowingly lost a match because the money was more lucrative. Who is to say they won't do it again? With e-sports getting more notable and popular, the possibility for corruption still heavily exists. You wouldn't let an alcoholic have one more drink would you?

Fixing is Cheating

Many try to separate match fixing and cheating into different categories, when they are truly the same thing, in reverse. You cheat, to win, to profit. You fix, to lose, to profit. While the steps you make take to get there are different, the end result is the same! The community needs to be vigilant and watching closer than ever...

Welcomed back?!

Tournament organizations are telling us, they don't care about the fans! The fact that any cheater or fixer is allowed to participate in ANY semi-professional (or higher) level event is outrageous. We are here to watch high-level, high-momentum CS:GO, not professional wrestling like your loser uncle.

I am calling on all leagues to re-evaluate their rules, and for Cloud9 to finally make the right choice to drop Brax from their organization!
